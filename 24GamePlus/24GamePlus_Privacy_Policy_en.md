# 24 Game+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: `[2026-03-07]`

`24 Game+` ("the App") highly values user privacy and personal information protection. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information, and what rights you have.

If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using the App.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to personal information processing activities when you use the App via the iOS client.

## 2. Information We Process

### 2.1 Local Functional Data (Not Uploaded by Default)

The App's core function is solving 24-point calculations. The numbers you enter, calculation process, and results are processed locally on your device by default and are not proactively uploaded to our own servers.

### 2.2 Local Storage

To ensure functionality, the App stores a small amount of settings data locally (for example, target number preferences) to restore your experience on next launch.

### 2.3 Clipboard

When you actively use the "Copy Result" feature, the App writes calculation results to the system clipboard only for your own paste actions.

### 2.4 Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The App integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK to display ads. Such third-party services may process certain information according to their own privacy rules, for example:

- Device identifiers (possibly including advertising identifiers)
- IP address and approximate location information
- Ad impressions, clicks, and interaction data
- Basic device and app information (such as OS version and app version)

The above information is processed by third-party service providers. We do not obtain or use such information beyond what is described in this policy.

## 3. System Permission Statement

The App declares several system permission descriptions in `Info.plist` (such as camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, Bluetooth, location, photos, and speech recognition). For the current app version, we do not proactively call these permissions for the core 24-point function.

If future versions add related features that require such permissions, we will:

- request your explicit consent through system prompts before actual access;
- update this Privacy Policy with specific purposes, scope, and processing methods.

You can disable granted permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Third-party services used by the App may include (subject to the actual released version):

- Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob)
- Google User Messaging Platform (UMP, introduced as an ad SDK dependency)

You may review third-party privacy policies:

- Google Privacy Policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- How Google uses data on partner sites/apps: <https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites>
- AdMob iOS Data Disclosure: <https://developers.google.com/admob/ios/privacy/data-disclosure>

## 5. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except under the following circumstances:

- with your explicit consent;
- where necessary for ad display and related functions, processed by third-party SDKs under their own rules;
- as required by laws, regulations, or competent authorities.

If data transfer is involved due to entity changes, merger, or split, we will protect your lawful rights and interests in accordance with applicable laws.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- Local functional data is mainly stored on your device;
- for data processed by third-party advertising services, storage location, retention period, and security measures are managed by the corresponding third party;
- we take reasonable measures to protect app and data security, but please understand that internet and mobile communication environments are not absolutely secure.

## 7. Protection of Minors

If you are a minor, please read and use the App under the guidance of your guardian. Guardians should supervise and guide minors' use behavior.

## 8. Your Rights

Within the scope permitted by applicable law, you may manage personal information by:

- disabling or restricting system permissions (iOS Settings);
- adjusting ad-related device settings (such as limiting personalized ads);
- stopping use of or uninstalling the App.

If you need to submit a privacy-related request, you can contact us through the "Contact Us" section of this policy.

## 9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy according to business features, legal requirements, or regulatory requirements. Updated versions will be published through in-app notices, app store pages, or other reasonable channels. For major changes, we will provide a more prominent notice.
