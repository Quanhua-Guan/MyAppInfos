# 24 Game Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-11  
Last Updated: 2026-03-11

Welcome to 24 Game. We value and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use 24 Game (the "App"), and what choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Trigger
- In-app purchase information: When you buy ad removal or hint credits, payment is processed by Apple App Store. We only receive purchase results needed to unlock features, and we do not receive your full card/payment details.
- Restore purchases information: When you use "Restore Purchases," we process Apple's response to determine your entitlement status.

### 1.2 Device and Usage Information (may be collected by third-party SDKs)
- Advertising-related information: The App integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). This service may collect device identifiers, IP address, device details, ad impressions/clicks, and related data for ad delivery, frequency control, anti-fraud, and measurement.
- Tracking authorization status: On iOS 14 and above, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission. You can allow or deny tracking.

### 1.3 Locally Stored Information
- Game progress and settings: such as hint balance, ad-removal status, game configuration, and play state, mainly stored on your device.
- Custom background image: when you choose a photo as background, it is stored locally on your device for display.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide core gameplay features (levels, hints, progress saving).
- Complete and restore in-app purchase entitlements.
- Display and manage ads (personalized or non-personalized, depending on your authorization and regional rules).
- Maintain reliability and security (for example, troubleshooting and abuse prevention).

## 3. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information.  
Information may be processed by the following third parties:
- Apple (App Store / StoreKit / Game Center): for payment, purchase verification, restore purchases, and Game Center features.
- Google (AdMob and related services): for ad serving and ad compliance.

Their privacy policies apply to their processing:
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Permissions

The App may declare or use these system permissions:
- Photo Library: to select a custom background image.
- Camera: for image-related features (if camera entry is enabled).
- Tracking (ATT): for ad personalization authorization.
- Other permissions (such as Bluetooth, Calendar, Contacts, Location, Microphone, Speech Recognition): requested only when related features are enabled and used.

You can manage permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Most app data is stored locally on your device and is generally removed when you uninstall the App (subject to iOS backup/system behavior).
- We use reasonable safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

If you are a minor, you should review this policy with a parent or guardian and use purchase-related features only with guardian consent.

## 7. Your Choices

You can:
- Enable or disable permissions (for example, Photos, Tracking) in iOS settings.
- Manage purchases/subscriptions in the App Store.
- Remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the App (subject to system behavior).

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy based on product changes or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date. For material changes, we will provide additional notice when appropriate.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions, feedback, or complaints about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the developer contact details on the App Store listing page.
